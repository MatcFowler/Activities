UNO, The timeless Classic.

Hello Im UnoTM the card game everyone is still raving about! this simple game is about matching either the pattern or number of the card as a turn, or using a special action card that can be used on any card. The object of the game is to be the first person to play all your cards, but there is a threat! Your friends are now your enemy, if you are down to one card and do not anounce UNO, they will be able to call it on you and force an additional 2 cards into your hand. There are 6 types of cards in UNO.

Quick Rules:

  1. Match the color
  2. Match the number
  3. Match the type
  4. wild or +4 cards can be played anytime
  5. call uno when down to one card, others can call it before you and you will have to take 2 cards
